Database:
---------
>> It is an organized collection of related data. It is easy to retrive, modify the data.

RDBMS --> Relational Database Management Systems 
-------
>> The data are stored in form of Tables(Rows, Cols).
>> It uses SQL.
Ex: MySQL, PostgreSQL, Oracle......

DBMS 
------
>> The data are stored in form of Documents(BSON).
Ex: MongoDB.
